Macaroni and Cheese

Description

Soft, delicious macaroni with creamy cheddar cheese

Ingredients

  • 2 cups macaroni
  • 1 cup cheddar cheese
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup milk

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil
  2. Add macaroni
  3. Boil uncovered for 10 minutes, stirring frequently
  4. Remove macaroni and strain
  5. Stir in cheese and milk
