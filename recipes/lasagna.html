Lasagna

Description

Rich, cheesy spinach lasagna

Ingredients

  • 1 box lasagna noodles
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 1 cup spinach
  • 1/2 cup cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Bring 4 cups of water to a boil
  2. Add lasagna noodles to water, cook uncovered for 10 minutes
  3. Remove and strain noodles
  4. Layer ricotta cheese, tomatoes, and spinach between lasagna noodles
  5. Top off with cheddar cheese
  6. Bake at 325 degrees for 10 minutes
